About Us

We are a prestigious cricket club that has been an active participant in various Cricket Leagues since 1852. Currently, we participate in the Hamilton and District Cricket League. With a rich history and a strong tradition of excellence, we strive to uphold the values of sportsmanship, teamwork, and camaraderie. Our dedicated players and passionate supporters make us a force to be reckoned with on the cricket field. The season for Cambridge Cricket Club runs from May to October, during which we engage in thrilling matches, fierce rivalries, and unforgettable moments. Join us as we continue to write our chapter in the storied legacy of cricket.
